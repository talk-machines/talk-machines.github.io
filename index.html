Abstract

This paper, titled "SayMove and TalkMachines: Large/Vision Language Models for Interpretable Robotic Manipulation and Perception to Enhance Human-Robot Interaction," explores the development and integration of advanced AI models for improving the interaction between humans and robots. The focus is on the control and perception modules of the TalkMachines system, which leverage large language models and vision-based technologies to execute commands and process environmental data effectively.
